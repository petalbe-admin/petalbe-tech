Our Work

Each app starts with a problem we had ourselves. Something we looked for, couldn't find done properly, and decided to build.

Mera — Hindi Vocabulary Companion

For people studying Hindi with a tutor or structured course.

  • Instead of working through generic word lists, Mera lets you upload your own vocabulary from lessons and practise exactly what matters.
  • Built around proven learning principles such as active recall and spaced repetition, expressed through flashcards and multiple choice exercises.
  • Works fully offline, so you can practise whenever you have a moment — without distraction and without needing a connection.

Language learning has been shown to support memory, attention, and cognitive flexibility over time. Mera makes that process focused, personal, and sustainable.

Luma — Fasting & Keto for Women In Development

For women who want to explore intermittent fating and ketogenic diet.

  • Luma provides structured 30-day protocols tailored to your goals, experience, and hormonal status — whether you are cycling, in perimenopause, or post-menopause.
  • Clear direction on when to fast, what to eat, and how to adjust over time, supported by a fasting tracker, personalised meal planning, and an AI coach that is always available when you need it.

Most fasting and nutrition guidance is built around male physiology. Luma takes a different approach — grounding its recommendations in female biology, where timing, recovery, and hormonal rhythms matter. Based on current research, it is designed to help you apply fasting in a way that works with your body, not against it.

Philosophy

The best apps work with the grain of human nature. We use science not as decoration, but as a starting point. And we draw on natural systems as a design compass: biological rhythms, ecological patterns, the way living things organise themselves.

Deep attention

The things worth doing like learning a language, understanding your body, knowing your materials, take time and focus. We build tools that reward that kind of attention.

Grounded in research

Every app we build starts with a body of work we actually read. Science shapes the features, the flow, and equally, what we choose not to build.

Built to last

We are not building for virality. We are building for people who will still be using an app a year from now because it genuinely helps.

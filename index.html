Current Projects

Mera — Hindi Vocabulary Trainer Now in Beta

Mera is a personalised language learning companion designed to support cognitive health and meaningful cultural connection through Hindi vocabulary acquisition.

Research shows that language learning can help preserve memory, attention, and mental flexibility as we age. Mera empowers learners to upload their own vocabulary lists, enabling focused practice aligned with classroom learning or personal study goals.

Built with a variety of evidence-based learning methods — including flashcards, multiple choice exercises, and progress tracking — Mera adapts to individual learning patterns. Fully functional offline, it respects your time and attention while fostering sustainable language development.

Luma — Adaptive Fasting & Wellness

Luma is a cycle-aware fasting and nutrition app designed to support metabolic health and hormonal balance for women.

Built on the principles of biological rhythm awareness, sustainability, and personal adaptability, Luma empowers users to develop a healthier relationship with food, energy, and their body.

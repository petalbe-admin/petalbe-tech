Why Mera exists

Built while learning.
Used every day.

When I started learning Hindi, I wanted the freedom to choose my own path: conversational Hindi first, script later. I also wanted to practice the exact words my teacher gave me. I couldn't find an app that worked that way. So I built Mera.

What makes Mera special

Built for the way
you actually learn.

Your vocabulary, not ours

Add words from your lessons, import a CSV, or start with our curated starter set. Mera works around your learning — not the other way around.

Varied exercises — and growing

Multiple exercise types keep practice fresh and effective. New types are added over time, and your vocabulary works across all of them automatically.

Choose your script

Roman script by default — so you can focus on the words, not the writing system. Devanagari coming as an opt-in for when you're ready.
